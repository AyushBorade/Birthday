## Required Photos

Add your photos with the following names:

- `photo1.jpg` - Main featured photo (will be large in the bento grid)
- `photo2.jpg` - Secondary photo
- `photo3.jpg` - Secondary photo
- `photo4.jpg` - Tall photo (works best with portrait orientation)
- `photo5.jpg` - Wide photo (works best with landscape orientation)

## Photo Recommendations

- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Resolution**: At least 800x800 pixels for best quality
- **Aspect Ratios**:
  - `photo1.jpg` - Square works best
  - `photo2.jpg`, `photo3.jpg` - Square or portrait
  - `photo4.jpg` - Portrait (tall)
  - `photo5.jpg` - Landscape (wide)

## Placeholder Behavior

If images are not found, the components will display elegant placeholder cards with heart icons. This allows you to preview the site before adding actual photos.

## How to Add Photos

1. Rename your photos to match the naming convention above
2. Copy them into this `public/assets/` folder
3. Refresh the page to see your photos
